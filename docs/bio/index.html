Biography

Spacebarman is an electronic/experimental music project, or the musical alter-ego of JP Carrascal, a Colombian-Spanish artist and researcher. He is sometimes joined by friends, including:

  • Mauricio: drums
  • Daniel: bass
  • Pablo: bass
  • Laura: background vocals

Its music is the result of an unholy mixture of dark electro pop, industrial, drum & bass and shoegaze. It is equally dance floor and moshpit-friendly. Its concerts are a digital art laboratory, where interactive experiences and reactive visuals created with the help of AI create an immersive experience for the audience.
